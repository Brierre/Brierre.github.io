Project One--Rehearsal Scheduler

This is Rehearsal Scheduler V1.0, a tool for musical and play directors to aid in creating a rehearsal calendar. Version 1.0 is a REST application built using Java, MySQL, and Spring Boot. I have big dreams for later versions of this project as I learn more languages and develop my front end skills. For now, it can be cloned and run in your Spring Tool Suite.

History of the World, Pt. Haigh

I'm old school. I grew up on The Oregon Trail and Odell Lake on the IBM computers at school and the atari 2600 at Gram's house when my cousins and I would stay over. I always laugh when I see Lorem Ipsum text because I wonder how many kids have actually been taught about Lorem Ipsum being filler text. I learned about it ages ago in school (before there was internet). We got our first computer at home in 1983 (ahead of the curve in my circle) and I spent every minute I could playing Lemonade, Moon Patrol, Donkey Kong, The Bard's Tale (I had a pirated 5.25" floppy and no map--I have since purchased the game!) and a bunch of text-based games that I wish I could find, or at least remember well enough to recreate!

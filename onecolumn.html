What's new? April 8, 2023

I spent this week taking short classes online in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to prepare for my Front End Developer Boot Camp through Promineo Tech and Gateway Community College in Arizona. I'll be studying several weeks concentrating on each language, but I had the time to take these mini courses through a site called GreatLearning, and they've been quite helpful. I'm looking forward to writing code in the front end program. After having finished their Back End Boot Camp, I was impressed by the community at Promineo Tech and wanted to go full stack.

My first boot camp was also through Promineo Tech, only the school was the Community College of Philadelphia, and it is pretty incredible just how much information I crammed into 18 weeks. I went from having faked my way through some Lua code a couple of years ago (written for a private FFXI open-source project) and having no idea what I was doing, to writing an entire REST application from scratch, using our homework and several other students' projects as models. I chose Gateway Community College this time around because 1.) I LOVE the desert. It's my happy place, and 2.) The only time I can take a class on Thursdays this semester is after 9pm, so the PST of Arizona this time of year worked out perfectly. I'm one of those people who believes in signs and to some extent, destiny. Arizona is definitely calling my name here!

When I began the back end boot camp, I had no intentions of looking for work in the field of software development, I simply wanted to learn to do a new skill the right way. Now that I have been able to create things and have seen the fruits of my labor, I am not entirely ruling out a career change. My current job is teaching choir, musical theatre, and general music in grades 6-12. I love my students and I love teaching music, so I'm not ready to jump in with both feet yet.
